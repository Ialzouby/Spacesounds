Predictions & Observations

The team predicted...

The light waves coming from the sun would be stronger and more varied than the “echo” from the moon. Team Sonic Sounds observed that the data was much more consistent in the sun than in the moon, even before the sonification process.

One day was not nearly enough time to gather data. The team has future plans to observe the data over the period of the full moon cycle and anticipate a demonstration of starting with a new moon with a value of 0 or the note middle C. As the moon approaches full, the value would be 8, representing High C or one octave. The sun will go much more slowly; the team anticipates relatively constant values as received October 7, 2023.
